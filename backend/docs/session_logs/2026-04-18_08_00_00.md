**Files created:**

  Sits between BF and CL: ≈ 133,533,122 (−1.2% vs CL).

## Open Questions For Mentors

### Q4 — BF prior basis
**Context:** Bornhuetter-Ferguson requires an a-priori ultimate per accident year.
**Current assumption:** Prior = ULT(AY2022) / dev0(AY2022) × dev0(AY_i). AY 2022 is
treated as the most mature year in the triangle.
**Question:** Is the mature-AY ratio an acceptable BF prior for this portfolio, or should we
use an ELR (expected loss ratio) derived from external exposure data?

### Q5 — Mack SE interpretation
**Context:** Mack SE is computed using process variance only (estimation variance excluded).
Portfolio-level SE uses root-sum-of-squares without AY correlation correction.
**Question:** Is this simplification acceptable for the hackathon presentation, or should we
implement the full Mack formula including estimation variance and cross-AY covariance?
